History of International Hotsprings

The International Grand Hotel was established in 1952. It is a two-story stone building in Japanese style, standing as a testament to half a century of providing lush greenery and the beauty of embracing Yangmingshan. For 73 years, it has silently served friends who once stayed on the mountain. Welcoming guests to its summit, it has endured nearly the average human lifespan—whether you have soaked in its weathered baths, been caught by its dragon-holding hands twice, or simply come to fulfill a childhood memory of your father bringing you here. The International Grand Hotel always remains like an old friend, effortlessly making you feel at home, even as it has transformed and grown. The sense of being a guest in one’s own home remains as familiar as ever…

Hot Spring Introduction

Located in the Beitou hot spring area, this resort boasts high-quality natural hot springs rich in various minerals, perfect for soaking and relaxation. The hot spring water undergoes regular testing and disinfection, allowing guests to enjoy therapeutic baths in both indoor and open-air pools.
