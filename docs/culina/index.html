Delicious Cuisine - Chinese Exquisite Cuisine

Our restaurant specializes in exquisite Chinese cuisine, blending local ingredients with classic cooking techniques, suitable for single dining, couple dinners, and group round table meals. The menu includes seafood, seasonal vegetables, exquisite side dishes, and soups, and can be customized according to the number of people and demand for a round table menu.

Round table cuisine (example)

-First plate: Cold plate platter -Hot dishes: Steamed Sea Fish, Oyster Sauce Lao Cai Xin, Three Cup Chicken -Soup: Seafood stew or Chinese stew -Staple food: rice or Fried Rice -Dessert: Seasonal fruits or sweet soup

If you need to book a round table or private room service, please inform the restaurant in advance when booking or checking in.
