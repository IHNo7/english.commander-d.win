Driving Directions

From downtown Taipei:

  1. Drive along Zhongshan North Road / Tai 5A (or follow navigation), turn onto Yangde Avenue or Xingyi Road, then proceed onto the main road of Yangmingshan towards Beitou / Yangmingshan.
  2. Follow the sign to turn into “Lake Mountain Road Section 1,” then drive along the road to reach No. 7. The hotel provides parking spaces (please confirm upon booking).

    Recommendation: During holiday periods, traffic may be restricted or congested on the Yangmingshan/Beitou route. Please check real-time traffic conditions before departure or consider leaving earlier.

    Public transport

    • Take the Red Line (Tamsui-Xinyi Line) of the MRT to Beitou Station (Beitou), then transfer to a bus bound for Yangmingshan (e.g., routes 230, 111, or the shuttle between the City Hall and Yangmingshan, subject to actual Taipei City bus routes). Alight near Hushan Road or at the Yangmingshan Terminal and walk to your destination.
    • If departing from Taipei Main Station, you can take the MRT to Beitou and then transfer to a bus or taxi to the hotel entrance.

    • If you are carrying luggage or arriving in the evening, taking a taxi or booking a hotel shuttle service would be more convenient.
